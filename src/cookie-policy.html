1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use a site.

First-party cookies are set by us. Third-party cookies are set by other companies (for example, analytics providers) whose services appear on our pages.

2. Why We Use Cookies

On the Website, we may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Operate and secure the Website
  • Remember basic preferences where applicable
  • Measure traffic and usage (analytics) so we can improve content and marketing

We do not use the Website to serve third-party advertising cookies for cross-site behavioral advertising.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. See help pages for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.
  • Analytics opt-out: Use Google’s opt-out add-on linked above, or block analytics cookies in your browser.

If you block or delete cookies, some parts of the Website may not function as intended.

Do Not Track: Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” (DNT) signal. There is no uniform industry standard for responding to DNT; we do not currently respond to DNT signals in a specific way.

5. Other Tracking Technologies

We may use similar technologies on the Website, such as local storage or pixels, for the same general purposes described above (operation and analytics). These technologies may store information in your browser or device rather than in a traditional cookie file.

We do not use Flash Local Shared Objects (LSOs) on the Website.

6. Mobile Apps

Our native iOS and Android applications generally do not use browser cookies in the same way as the Website. The apps may use mobile analytics and identifiers (for example, Firebase Analytics) as described in our Privacy Policy. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (e.g., “Limit Ad Tracking” on iOS or advertising settings on Android).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates when it was last revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
