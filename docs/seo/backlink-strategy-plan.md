# Bit Forge Strength Backlink Strategy Plan

## Objective

Increase high-quality referring domains and relevant backlinks to improve visibility in Google and Bing for non-brand terms.

## Success Metrics

- New referring domains per month: 8-15
- Editorial backlinks from relevant sites: 4-8 per month
- Organic impressions for non-brand queries: +20% in 60-90 days
- Referral traffic from outreach placements: +10% in 60 days

## Link Quality Standard

Prioritize links that are:

- Topically relevant (fitness, strength, coaching, AI productivity, health tech)
- Editorial (inside article content), not just profile pages
- On pages that are indexed and updated
- From unique domains (avoid multiple links from same site early)

Avoid:

- Paid link schemes, private blog networks, bulk directory spam, low-quality guest-post farms

## 8-Week Execution Plan

### Phase 1 (Week 1): Build Link-Worthy Assets

Create 2-4 assets people can cite:

1. Home Gym Equipment Compatibility Guide (free)
2. Strength Progression Calculator or downloadable sheet
3. Beginner-to-Intermediate 12-week progression blueprint
4. AI Workout Planning methodology page with examples

Requirements for each asset:

- Unique URL
- Clear headline and practical value
- Shareable chart/table/checklist
- Clear source citations where needed

### Phase 2 (Week 2): Foundation Links

Claim and optimize core listings:

- Company social profiles (LinkedIn, X, YouTube)
- Startup and product directories (Crunchbase, Product Hunt if active)
- Relevant fitness software directories and communities
- Founder profile pages linking to main site

### Phase 3 (Weeks 3-4): Targeted Outreach

Build list of 50-80 prospects in these categories:

- Strength coaches with blogs/newsletters
- Fitness tech reviewers
- Home gym and training media
- Startup and indie communities discussing AI tools

Execution:

- Send 10-15 personalized emails per week
- Offer one specific value item per pitch (data point, guest post, quote, co-created resource)
- Follow up once after 5-7 days

### Phase 4 (Weeks 5-6): Partnerships and Co-Marketing

Create 3-5 collaboration opportunities:

- Co-branded training guide
- Joint webinar or Q&A
- Case study with coach or gym
- Expert roundup article

Target outcome:

- 1-2 backlinks per collaboration partner

### Phase 5 (Weeks 7-8): Micro Digital PR

Publish one data-backed story:

- Example: common workout consistency patterns from aggregated user behavior
- Pitch to niche journalists, newsletters, and podcast hosts

Target outcome:

- 3-8 mentions/backlinks from one campaign

## Weekly Workflow

1. Monday: Prospect research and list updates
2. Tuesday: Draft and send outreach
3. Wednesday: Content production (linkable assets)
4. Thursday: Follow-ups and partnership calls
5. Friday: KPI review and next-week planning

## Anchor Text Guidance

Use a natural mix:

- 60-70% brand anchors (Bit Forge Strength, bitforgestrength.app)
- 20-30% topical natural anchors (AI workout planning app, strength training platform)
- <10% exact-match anchors

## Notes

Backlink gains usually lag 2-8 weeks before visible ranking impact. Consistency and relevance matter more than volume.
