# 30-Day Linkable Content Calendar (Bit Forge Strength)

## Goal

Publish 4 high-value assets in 30 days that can be pitched for backlinks and reused in outreach.

## Publishing Cadence

- 1 major asset per week
- 2 supporting distribution tasks per asset
- Ongoing outreach and follow-up every week

## Week 1 (Days 1-7)

### Asset 1: Home Gym Equipment Compatibility Guide

Format:

- Long-form guide with comparison table

Working title:

- Home Gym Equipment Guide: What You Need for Effective Strength Progression

Target keywords:

- home gym strength training equipment
- best equipment for strength progression

Promotion tasks:

1. Share with home gym communities and subreddits where permitted
2. Pitch to 10 home gym blogs as a resource inclusion

---

## Week 2 (Days 8-14)

### Asset 2: Strength Progression Calculator (or downloadable spreadsheet)

Format:

- Interactive calculator or downloadable template with instructions

Working title:

- Strength Progression Calculator: Build Your Next 12 Weeks in Minutes

Target keywords:

- strength progression calculator
- progressive overload plan template

Promotion tasks:

1. Publish short walkthrough post on LinkedIn/X
2. Pitch to 15 coaching blogs and newsletters

---

## Week 3 (Days 15-21)

### Asset 3: AI Workout Planning Framework (with examples)

Format:

- Methodology page with before/after sample plans

Working title:

- AI Workout Planning That Actually Works: A Coach-Friendly Framework

Target keywords:

- ai workout planner
- ai strength training plan

Promotion tasks:

1. Outreach to fitness tech publications (10 targets)
2. Offer expert quote contribution tied to this framework

---

## Week 4 (Days 22-30)

### Asset 4: Data Insight Post (micro digital PR)

Format:

- Data-backed article with 3-5 clear findings and visuals

Working title:

- What Training Consistency Data Reveals About Missed Workouts

Target keywords:

- workout consistency data
- training adherence insights

Promotion tasks:

1. Pitch to 20 journalists/newsletters/podcasts in fitness and wellness
2. Repurpose into infographic snippets for social

## Weekly Outreach Targets

- New prospects added: 15+
- Personalized emails sent: 10-15
- Follow-ups sent: 5-10
- Positive replies: 2-4

## Simple Production Checklist (for each asset)

1. Clear problem statement in first 2 paragraphs
2. One unique visual (table/chart/checklist)
3. Internal links to relevant product/feature pages
4. One CTA for newsletter or beta signup
5. Outreach blurb written in 3 sentence format

## End-of-Month Review

Track:

- Referring domains gained
- Backlinks earned per asset
- Referral traffic by source
- Non-brand impressions and clicks

Decision rules:

- Double down on asset format with best link rate
- Retire topics with low outreach response
- Reuse best-performing angle in month 2 campaign
